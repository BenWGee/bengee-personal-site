Things we need:

- One copy of cleansing wildfire
- Two mana available on turn two
- A wild land

Variables:
- Draw vs play
- Number of wild lands
- Number of wild lands that enter tapped vs untapped

Constants:

- Deck is 60 cards
- 4 copies of Cleansing Wildfire
- Always draw 7 card opener
